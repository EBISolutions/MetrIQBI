# MetrIQBI

## ❓ FAQ

<details>
<summary>What is MetriQ BI?</summary>

MetriQ BI is a Business Intelligence solution built on Microsoft Power BI that connects to Odoo ERP. It extracts and consolidates data (sales, purchases, inventory, accounting, etc.) and presents it through interactive dashboards and key performance indicators (KPIs) to support data analysis and decision-making.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Who is MetriQ BI for?</summary>
MetriQ BI is designed for companies that want to start or take their first steps into the world of Business Intelligence. It is ideal for organizations looking to leverage the value of their data but that may not yet have the resources, infrastructure, or expertise required to implement a BI solution from scratch.
MetriQ BI provides a solid and structured foundation to begin making data-driven decisions in an accessible and efficient way.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I need technical knowledge to use MetriQ BI?</summary>
No. MetriQ BI is designed so that business users can easily interpret information without requiring advanced technical knowledge.
The dashboards and reports are visual, intuitive, and easy to use, allowing executives, managers, and operational teams to quickly and effectively analyze information.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the business model of MetriQ BI?</summary>
MetriQ BI is offered through a monthly or annual subscription, with plans starting from 10 users.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is included in my MetriQ BI subscription?</summary>
Your subscription includes:
A custom connector that extracts data from Odoo ERP into Microsoft Power BI
ETL processes for data extraction and transformation
A structured data architecture that organizes information into an optimized analytical data model
The cost of the Power BI Pro license
Maintenance and user administration within Power BI
Access to reports developed by a specialized business intelligence and data analytics team*
Ongoing training on how to use the platform**
</details>

<details>
<summary>Which versions of Odoo are compatible with MetriQ BI?</summary>
MetriQ BI is compatible with Odoo Enterprise v16 and later, whether deployed:
- On-premise
- On Odoo.sh
- On Odoo Online
</details>

<details>
<summary>I already have a BI team. Can they create their own reports?</summary>
Yes. MetriQ BI structures and models the information extracted from Odoo so your analytics team can easily create new reports, dashboards, and custom analyses using Microsoft Power BI.
This allows your team to work with organized and reliable data, reducing the time spent preparing information and focusing instead on generating valuable business insights.
MetriQ BI is designed to enhance your BI team's capabilities, not replace them.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use MetriQ BI with other ERPs or data sources?</summary>
Not at the moment. MetriQ BI is currently designed to work with Odoo Enterprise data.
However, if you require a solution integrated with another ERP or data source, feel free to contact us to discuss possible options.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Do all my Odoo users need a MetriQ BI subscription?</summary>
No. Only users who are involved in data analysis, report creation, or business decision-making need a MetriQ BI subscription.
</details>
